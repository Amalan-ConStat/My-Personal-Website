  4. There is only one action movie for general audiences(for all) and obviously no horror film should be watched by children alone, yet there are 7 movies which you can watch with your parents.
  5. Comedy movies are mostly R rated(under 17 requires guardian) and PG-13 (some material is inappropriate to under 13). The frequencies are respectively 367 and 328.
  6. Where 309 movies of adventure genre could be watched by children with accompanying parents and most(67) number of adventure movies can be watched by all ages. Yet 645 Drama movies are R-rated.
  7. We think horror movies are mostly R-rated then it is true. But only is relative to percentage because considering the frequencies we make horror movies the least even according to this random sample.
